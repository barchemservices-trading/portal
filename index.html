ABOUT US

Barchem was established in 2002 for trading and general contracting. Since its inception, Barchem has successfully performed all awarded projects in maintenance, disinfection, pest control, construction services, and the distribution of safety devices.

Barchem has gained the unwavering trust of its clients, with its name becoming a hallmark of credibility and professionalism.
